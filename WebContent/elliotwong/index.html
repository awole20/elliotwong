Elliot's work explores different conceptions of physical space and presents various viewpoints of the expanse in which we live, including both traditional depictions of our world, as well as abstract renditions. He has been influenced by a wide assortment of styles, encompassing Impressionism, Cubism, Surrealism, and Abstract Expressionism, and figures ranging from Berthe Morisot and Picasso to Joan Mitchell and Sherrie Franssen.

Elliot graduated in 2008 from the University of California, Davis and is currently studying at University of California, Hastings. He lives in San Francisco.

All questions and inquiries can be sent to elliot at elliotwong dot org.
